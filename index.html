About SPARK

🎯 Our Mission

To empower students to become compassionate leaders who create positive change in their communities through meaningful service, advocacy, and social action. We believe that every small act of kindness can spark a movement of transformation.

👁 Our Vision

A world where young people are equipped with the tools, passion, and platform to address social challenges and build stronger, more inclusive communities. We envision a future where service is not just an activity, but a way of life.

💫 Our Motto

"Serving People and Raising Kindness" - This motto embodies our commitment to putting people first, fostering empathy, and creating ripple effects of positive change that extend far beyond our immediate community.
